🌆 Popular Cities to Stay In

Thailand’s diversity means you can sleep by the beach, in the jungle, or in a rooftop condo in the heart of Bangkok.

  • Bangkok – modern hotels, nightlife, and BTS access
  • Chiang Mai – digital nomad haven with cafés & coworking
  • Phuket & Krabi – beach resorts and remote work by the sea
  • Hua Hin – slower pace, ideal for long-stay retirees
  • Pattaya / Koh Samui – expat-friendly coastal towns

🛏 Types of Accommodation to Consider

  • 🏢 Condos & Serviced Apartments – great for long stays
  • 🏨 Boutique Hotels – stylish, local, and budget-friendly
  • 🏝️ Beach Resorts – all-inclusives or remote bungalows
  • 🛌 Budget Guesthouses – community-style travel
  • 🏠 Agoda Homes / Booking’s apartments – hybrid Airbnb vibe
