Monthly Rentals in Thailand

Welcome to the Rent section!

Here you’ll find information on long-stay rentals — from monthly condo options to serviced apartments across Thailand, ideal for digital nomads, retirees, or long-term expats.

🏡 Find Your Home in Thailand

Looking to stay in Thailand for a month or more? Whether you're working remotely, escaping the winter, or exploring your retirement options — finding a good long-term rental is key.

This guide helps you compare monthly rental platforms, popular areas, and tips for getting the best deal.

📍 Popular Cities for Long-Stay Rentals

  • Bangkok — modern condos, co-living, BTS-friendly
  • Chiang Mai — quiet, affordable, great for digital nomads
  • Hua Hin — perfect for retirees, beachside condos
  • Phuket & Krabi — scenic, island vibes with a tourist twist

💡 Money-Saving Tips

  • 💬 Negotiate directly if staying 1+ month (especially on Airbnb or Facebook)
  • 📉 Avoid high season (Nov–Feb) to get better deals
  • 🏢 Look outside tourist zones for quiet and cheaper neighborhoods
