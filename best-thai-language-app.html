Best Thai Language Apps for Expats, Nomads & Retirees

🏆 Best Apps to Learn Thai for Expats, Nomads & Retirees

Learning Thai doesn’t have to be hard. Whether you want to order food, chat with locals, or navigate daily life — these tools will help you speak with confidence.

Here’s how the top Thai learning apps compare:

🧠 Ling App – Best All-in-One Structured Thai Course

👉 Explore Ling App

✅ Thai script, grammar, and culture

✅ Chatbot, quizzes, and spaced repetition

✅ Designed specifically for Southeast Asia learners

💰 Around $6–9/month

Excellent choice for expats or long-term visitors.

🏑 Mondly – Best for Quick, Fun Thai Basics

👉 Try Mondly Here

✅ Great for total beginners

✅ Learn Thai phrases with speech recognition

❌ Limited grammar and Thai script

💰 $10–15/month

Perfect for new arrivals or casual learners who want to speak some Thai quickly.

🎧 Pimsleur Thai – Best for Audio Learners

👉 Try Pimsleur Thai

✅ Focused on speaking and listening

✅ Practice conversations hands-free

❌ No reading or writing

💰 One-time or monthly plans ($20–$150)

Great if you're always on the move or driving.

📚 ThaiPod101 – Best for Mixed-Skill Learners

👉 Check ThaiPod101

✅ Hundreds of video and audio lessons

✅ Covers script, grammar, and culture

✅ New content regularly

💰 Free with optional paid plans

Solid all-around option for structured learners and self-starters.

🎥 Learn Thai with Mod – Best Free YouTube Resource

👉 Watch Learn Thai with Mod

✅ Real-world Thai phrases, etiquette, and tone

✅ Great cultural insight

✅ Free and beginner-friendly

❌ No progress tracking or interactivity

Perfect for visual learners and cultural immersion.

📝 Final Thoughts

If you're just starting out, Mondly is a great entry point. But if you're planning to stay in Thailand longer, we recommend combining Mondly with something deeper like Ling App or ThaiPod101.

💡 Pro tip: Choose one app to build your routine, and supplement with YouTube or Pimsleur to strengthen specific skills.
