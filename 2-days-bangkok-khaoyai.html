2-Day Khao Yai Itinerary from Bangkok: Nature, Wine & Relaxation

🏞️ 2-Day Khao Yai Itinerary from Bangkok

Escape the hustle of Bangkok and enjoy nature, wine, and cool cafes in just a weekend. Here’s a relaxed and scenic 2-day itinerary to make the most of your Khao Yai trip.

📅 Day 1: Nature & Wine

🚗 Morning: Depart Bangkok Early

  • Leave around 6:30–7:00 AM to beat traffic
  • Arrive in Khao Yai ~9:30 AM

🏞️ Late Morning: Khao Yai National Park

  • Visit Haew Suwat Waterfall (famous from The Beach)
  • Optional short hikes or viewpoint at Pha Diew Dai
  • Look out for wild elephants, gibbons, and hornbills
    🎫 Park Entry Fee: 400 THB (foreigners), 40 THB (Thai nationals)

🍽️ Lunch:

  • Local Thai restaurants outside the park

🍷 Afternoon: PB Valley Winery Tour

  • Vineyard tour + wine tasting
  • Alternative: GranMonte for boutique vibes

☕ Late Afternoon: Scenic Cafe

  • Yellow Submarine Coffee Tank or Pirom Cafe

🏨 Evening: Check-in & Dinner

  • Recommended Stays:
  • Sala Khao Yai, Thames Valley, Botanica, Muthi Maya
  • Dinner Options: Midwinter Green or hotel restaurant

📅 Day 2: Leisure & Local Charm

🐮 Morning: Animal Encounters

  • Farm Chokchai (family-friendly, cowboy theme)
    or
  • Primo Piazza (alpacas + Tuscany-style scenery)

🌸 Late Morning: Flower Fields

  • Visit The Bloom or seasonal gardens

🛍️ Lunch & Souvenirs

  • Lunch at local café or Pak Chong Market
  • Buy wine jelly, grapes, or local treats

🛣️ Afternoon: Return to Bangkok

  • Leave around 3:00 PM to avoid evening traffic
  • Optional coffee stop en route

✅ Tips

  • Wear comfy shoes and bring insect repellent
  • Book winery tours in advance
  • Carry cash for local markets and entry tickets

📌 Perfect for:

  • Couples, small families, or anyone who wants a quick mix of nature, food, and culture close to Bangkok.
