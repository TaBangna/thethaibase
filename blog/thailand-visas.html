Thailand Visa Types (2025 Edition)

Here's how to choose the right visa for your lifestyle in Thailand — whether you're retiring, working remotely, or moving with family.

Popular Visa Options

  • Tourist Visa (TR): 60-day visits, extendable
  • Retirement Visa (O/OA): Age 50+, proof of income or deposit
  • LTR Visa: Long-Term Residency for wealthy expats or digital workers
  • Education Visa: Learn Thai, Muay Thai, or attend school
