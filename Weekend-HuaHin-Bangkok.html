Weekend Hua Hin Itinerary from Bangkok: Beaches, Cafes & Culture

🌊 Weekend Hua Hin Itinerary from Bangkok

Just a few hours from Bangkok, Hua Hin is the perfect destination for a weekend filled with sun, sea, markets, and cultural experiences. Here's a 2-day itinerary ideal for couples, families, or solo travelers looking to unwind.

📅 Day 1: Coastal Charm & Local Flavors

🚗 Morning: Travel to Hua Hin

  • Depart Bangkok by car, train, or van around 7:00 AM.
  • Travel time: ~2.5–3 hours.

🏖️ Late Morning: Hit the Beach

  • Relax at Hua Hin Beach or Khao Takiab Beach.
  • Try horseback riding or just lounge under a beach umbrella.

🍽️ Lunch:

  • Enjoy seafood at Chaolay Seafood or Let's Sea Restaurant.

🛍️ Afternoon: Explore Local Markets

  • Visit the Hua Hin Market Village (shopping & snacks).
  • Check in at your hotel and rest before the evening.

🌙 Evening: Night Market Fun

  • Head to Cicada Market or Tamarind Market for food, art, and live music (Fri–Sun).
  • Try local Thai dishes, handmade goods, and enjoy the lively atmosphere.

📅 Day 2: Culture & Café Hopping

🌄 Morning: Temples & Views

  • Visit Wat Khao Takiab (Monkey Mountain) or Wat Huay Mongkol (Luang Phor Thuad statue).
  • Catch panoramic views of the coastline.

☕ Late Morning: Coffee Break

  • Visit a scenic café like Air Space, Seenspace, or Baan Gliwang.

🛍️ Lunch & Local Treats

  • Grab lunch at a local seafood place or The Tea House for something cozy.
  • Pick up snacks or souvenirs from local markets.

🚗 Afternoon: Return to Bangkok

  • Depart Hua Hin around 3:00–4:00 PM.
  • Optional stop at Santorini Park or FN Outlet on the way back.

✅ Travel Tips

  • Book accommodation in advance during weekends or holidays.
  • Use Grab or local taxis if not driving.
  • Bring sun protection and light clothing.

📌 Ideal For:

Anyone seeking a short, rejuvenating escape with a mix of beach time, culture, and shopping — all within easy reach of Bangkok.
